Quizbowl Scorekeeping Web App

While volunteering to help manage Quizbowl tournaments for our school, I identified a need to to build an app to manage the scorekeeping and reporting (previously all done on paper). I built this site to allow for score to be kept on laptops/tablets with real-time scores, standings, and stats reporting which could be viewable by all the participants on mobile devices. This app has been used for the past two years at our tournaments with rave reviews and is still undergoing development to increase its capabilities. Here are some of the highlights of the site design:

  • Custom backend engine to manage score entry, stats calculation, rankings, admin features, etc.
  • Database structure allows for archiving of all tournament information
  • User login portal with various levels of access assignments (i.e. public, participant, staff, admin)
  • Real-time score, stats, and ranking reporting
  • Looping projector page to display current standings and stats for the public gathering room
