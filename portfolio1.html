Mountain Vista Governor's School Website

While working at the school, I took on the responsibility of designing and coding the school website. The site is a primary means of delivering information to the community, parents, students, and prospective students. Here are some of the highlights of the design:

  • Clean, minimalist design (desired by the administration)
  • Responsive design to present information appropriately on all devices
  • Well-organized, dynamic menus
  • Admin console allowing non-technical staff to manage news cards
  • Special admin mobile-first page to allow easy one-click snowday alert banners to be published to the home page.
  • Embedded forms on Foundation page allowing for PayPal donations
  • Embedded SmugMug photo gallery allowing non-technical staff to manage picture slideshow
