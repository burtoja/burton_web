DC Mystery Website

I developed a school-wide activity in which students travel to Washington D.C. to collect clues to break codes and solve a mystery. I built a mobile friendly site from the ground up to handle delivering the clues, codes, etc. to the students throughout the day. Students interact with the site to get real-time feedback on their progress in siolving the mystery and to keep an eye on the progress of the other teams. Here are some of the highlights of the site design:

  • Mobile first, clean design that reads well when viewed outside on a sunny days
  • User login and authentication
  • Dynamic forms for entering clue answers with immediate feedback
  • Embedded video clips
  • Real-time scoreboard
  • Admin portal for game management functions
